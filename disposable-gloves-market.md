<p><p>Disposable gloves market consists of three main types: Latex, Vinyl, and Nitrile. Latex gloves are made from natural rubber and offer excellent comfort and dexterity, but may cause allergic reactions in some individuals. Vinyl gloves are a cost-effective option that provide basic protection but are less durable than latex or nitrile gloves. Nitrile gloves are resistant to chemicals and punctures, making them a popular choice for medical and industrial use.
