<p><p>Edge computing technologies consist of three main market types: Cloud Computing, Compute Edge, and Device Edge. Cloud Computing involves processing data in remote servers over the internet. Compute Edge involves processing data on local servers or gateways closer to the data source. Device Edge involves processing data directly on the device itself. Each market type offers unique benefits and challenges, depending on factors such as latency requirements, security concerns, and bandwidth limitations.</p></p>
