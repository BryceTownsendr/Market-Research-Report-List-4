<p><p>Handheld tonometers are devices used to measure intraocular pressure in the eyes. There are various types of handheld tonometers available in the market like applanation tonometry which measures pressure by flattening a small area of the cornea, indentation tonometry which measures pressure by indenting the cornea, rebound tonometry which measures pressure by the rebound of a probe against the cornea, and other types which use different methods to determine intraocular pressure. These different types cater to the specific needs of healthcare professionals and patients.</p></p>
