Non-clog deep-well pumps are specifically designed to handle large solids without clogging, making them ideal for wastewater and sewage applications. Openwell deep-well pumps are installed in open wells to draw water for agricultural or industrial use. Borewell deep-well pumps are designed for use in boreholes to extract water from underground sources. Each type of deep-well pump serves a specific purpose and is chosen based on the intended application.</p></p>
