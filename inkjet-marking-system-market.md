<p><p>The inkjet marking system market is primarily divided into two types: Continuous Inkjet (CIJ) and Drop on Demand (DOD). CIJ systems are suitable for high-speed production lines due to their continuous printing capability. On the other hand, DOD systems are more commonly used for smaller batch sizes and variable data printing requirements. Both types of systems are widely utilized in various industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceutical, and packaging for coding, marking, and labeling products.</p></p>
