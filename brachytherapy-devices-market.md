<p><p>Brachytherapy devices market is categorized into high dose rate brachytherapy and low dose rate brachytherapy. High dose rate brachytherapy involves delivering a high dose of radiation over a short period, typically minutes, directly to the tumor site. Low dose rate brachytherapy, on the other hand, involves delivering radiation at a slower rate over a longer period, often days. Both types of brachytherapy are effective in treating various cancers, with the choice between them depending on the specific characteristics of the cancer being treated.</p></p>
