<p><ul><li>Bottled Water Dispenser or Bottled Water Cooler (BWC)</li><li>Point-of-Use (POU)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Water dispensers come in two main types: Bottled Water Dispenser or Bottled Water Cooler (BWC) and Point-of-Use (POU) dispensers. BWCs require bottled water to be placed on top of the dispenser, providing cold or hot water. On the other hand, POU dispensers are connected directly to the water supply and filter and dispense water on demand. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the market, offering convenience and accessibility for consumers looking for a reliable water source.</p></p>
