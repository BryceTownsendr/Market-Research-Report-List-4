<p><p>Doppler Lidar technology is utilized in both Foundation Type and Cabin Type markets. Foundation Type Doppler Lidar systems are ground-based and fixed in one location, commonly used for meteorological and environmental monitoring. On the other hand, Cabin Type Doppler Lidar systems are mounted on moving platforms such as airplanes or ships, allowing for more mobile applications like wind profiling and turbulence detection. Both market types cater to different needs and use cases within the Doppler Lidar industry.</p></p>
