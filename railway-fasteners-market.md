<p><p>Railway fasteners market can be classified into bolt-type and non-bolt type fasteners. Bolt-type fasteners include rail bolts, rail screw spikes, and rail nuts, which are used for joining rails and plates. On the other hand, non-bolt type fasteners include clips, clamps, and plates, which are used for holding the rails in place. Both types of fasteners are essential for maintaining the stability and safety of railway tracks, ensuring smooth and efficient train operations.</p></p>
