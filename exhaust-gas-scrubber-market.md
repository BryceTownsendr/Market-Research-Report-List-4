<p><p>An exhaust gas scrubber is a technology used to reduce harmful emissions from exhaust gases produced by ships and industrial processes. It works by spraying a alkaline solution (usually water with sodium hydroxide) into the exhaust gas stream, which reacts with and neutralizes pollutants such as sulfur dioxide and nitrogen oxides. This process results in cleaner emissions being released into the atmosphere.</p><p>The Exhaust Gas Scrubber Market is experiencing significant growth, with a projected CAGR of 12.4% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to a variety of factors, including increasing regulations on emissions from ships and industrial processes, as well as growing awareness of the environmental impact of these pollutants. Additionally, advancements in technology and the development of more efficient and cost-effective scrubber systems are driving market growth.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the exhaust gas scrubber market include the development of hybrid scrubber systems that combine both open and closed loop technologies, as well as the increasing adoption of exhaust gas cleaning systems by shipping companies to comply with stricter environmental regulations.
