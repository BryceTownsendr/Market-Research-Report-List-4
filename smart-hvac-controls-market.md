<p><p>Short-range Smart HVAC Controls refer to devices that have a limited range of communication, typically within a confined area like a single building or home. These controls are designed for localized control and monitoring of heating, ventilation, and air conditioning systems. On the other hand, Long-distance Smart HVAC Controls are capable of communicating over larger distances, allowing for remote monitoring and control of HVAC systems across multiple buildings or even cities. Both types of controls offer improved energy efficiency, convenience, and flexibility in managing HVAC systems.</p></p>
