<p><p>Mechanical ship unloaders come in two main types: mobile and floor track. Mobile ship unloaders are versatile and can be easily moved around the port to accommodate different docking areas. Floor track ship unloaders are fixed in place and move along a track system to reach different areas of the ship for unloading. The choice between mobile and floor track ship unloaders depends on the specific needs of the port and the frequency of ship unloading operations. Both types offer efficient and reliable solutions for bulk cargo handling.</p></p>
