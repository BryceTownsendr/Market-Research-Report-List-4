<p><ul><li>Single Junction</li><li>Multi-junction</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Amorphous silicon solar cells are classified into two main market types: Single Junction and Multi-junction. Single Junction cells consist of one layer of amorphous silicon, making them cost-effective and suitable for low to medium power applications. In contrast, Multi-junction cells are made up of multiple layers of amorphous silicon, allowing for higher efficiency and better performance under low light conditions. While Single Junction cells are more commonly used in commercial and residential applications, Multi-junction cells are typically used in specialized applications such as satellites and spacecraft.</p></p>
