<p><strong>The Low Voltage Relay Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Voltage Relay</li><li>Static Voltage Relay</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low voltage relays are classified into two main types: Electromagnetic Voltage Relay and Static Voltage Relay. </p><p>Electromagnetic Voltage Relays operate using an electromagnetic coil to open or close the circuit based on the voltage level. These relays are commonly used for applications that require high precision and reliability.</p><p>Static Voltage Relays, on the other hand, use solid-state semiconductor devices to control the circuit based on the voltage level. These relays are known for their fast response times and long lifespan, making them ideal for high-speed or high-frequency applications.</p></p>
