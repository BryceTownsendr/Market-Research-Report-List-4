<p><ul><li>Straight Bevel Gears</li><li>Spiral Bevel Gear</li><li>Zerol bevel gears</li><li>Hypoid bevel gears</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bevel gears are classified into different types based on their tooth configuration and design. Straight bevel gears have straight teeth and are commonly used in applications that require high speed and efficiency. Spiral bevel gears have curved teeth for smoother operation and higher load capacity. Zerol bevel gears have a unique tooth profile that combines the characteristics of straight and spiral bevel gears. Hypoid bevel gears have offset axes and are used in applications where the input and output shafts need to be at different angles. Each type of bevel gear caters to specific market needs and preferences.</p></p>
