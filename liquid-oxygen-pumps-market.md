<p><ul><li>Centrifugal Liquid Oxygen Pump</li><li>Reciprocating Liquid Oxygen Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Liquid Oxygen Pumps market can be categorized into two main types based on their working mechanisms – Centrifugal Liquid Oxygen Pump and Reciprocating Liquid Oxygen Pump. Centrifugal pumps use rotating impellers to create a vacuum that draws in the liquid oxygen and then pushes it out through a discharge pipe. On the other hand, Reciprocating pumps use a piston-like mechanism to pull in and push out the liquid oxygen. Both types of pumps are essential in various industries where liquid oxygen is used for different applications.</p></p>
