<p><ul><li>Solid Pendulum</li><li>Liquid Pendulum</li><li>Gas Pendulum</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Tilt sensors come in various types including solid pendulum, liquid pendulum, and gas pendulum. Solid pendulum tilt sensors use a solid mass suspended within a casing to measure tilt angles. Liquid pendulum sensors, on the other hand, use a liquid-filled chamber to detect tilting movements. Gas pendulum sensors utilize a gas-filled chamber to detect changes in tilt. Each type of tilt sensor has its unique advantages and applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and robotics.</p></p>
