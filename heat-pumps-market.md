<p><p>Heat pumps are a popular heating and cooling solution that come in various types to suit different needs. Air to water monobloc heat pumps are compact units that extract heat from the air and transfer it to water for heating. Air to water cylinder heat pumps use a large cylinder to store hot water. Ground/water to water heat pumps extract heat from the ground or water sources. Air to water split heat pumps have an outdoor unit and indoor unit for efficient heating. Exhaust air heat pumps recover heat from outgoing air for energy efficiency.</p></p>
