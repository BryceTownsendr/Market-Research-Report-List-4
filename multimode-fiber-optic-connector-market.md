<p><p>A multimode fiber optic connector is a type of optical connector that is used to connect two multimode fiber cables together for data transmission. Multimode fiber optic connectors have a larger core size than single-mode fiber optic connectors, allowing multiple modes of light to travel through the fiber simultaneously. This makes them ideal for short-distance data transmission in applications such as LANs, data centers, and enterprise networks.</p><p>The multimode fiber optic connector market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 10.1% during the forecast period.
