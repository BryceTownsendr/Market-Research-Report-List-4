<p><p>Double layer supercapacitors are divided into three types based on the electrode material used: Activated Carbon, Carbon Fiber, and Carbon Aerogel. Activated Carbon electrode materials are widely used for their high surface area and low cost. Carbon Fiber electrode materials offer high conductivity and mechanical strength. Carbon Aerogel electrode materials provide both high surface area and high conductivity. Each type of electrode material caters to different performance requirements and applications in the supercapacitor market, offering a range of options for manufacturers and end-users.</p></p>
