<p><strong>The Quartz Pressure Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Piezoresistive Quartz Pressure Sensor</li><li>Piezoelectric Quartz Pressure Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Quartz pressure sensors are classified into two main types: piezoresistive and piezoelectric. Piezoresistive quartz pressure sensors use a resistive element to measure pressure changes, while piezoelectric quartz pressure sensors generate an electric charge in response to pressure variations. Both types offer high accuracy and reliability in pressure measurements, making them ideal for various applications in industries such as automotive, aerospace, and medical. The choice between the two types of sensors depends on the specific requirements of the application in terms of sensitivity, response time, and environmental factors.</p></p>
