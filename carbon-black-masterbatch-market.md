<p><p>Carbon Black Masterbatch comes in various types based on the polymer resins it is designed to work with. PE Carbon Black Masterbatch is specifically formulated for use with polyethylene resins, while PP Carbon Black Masterbatch is designed for polypropylene resins. PS Carbon Black Masterbatch is tailored for polystyrene resins. Other types of Carbon Black Masterbatch are available for use with different types of polymer resins. Each type is optimized to provide the best dispersion of carbon black pigment within its respective resin.</p></p>
