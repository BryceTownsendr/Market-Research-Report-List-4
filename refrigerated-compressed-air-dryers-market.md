<p><p>Refrigerated compressed air dryers are used in various industries to remove moisture from compressed air to prevent damage to equipment and products. The two main types of refrigerated air dryers are cycling and non-cycling. Cycling refrigerated air dryers use a refrigeration compressor that turns on and off based on demand, offering energy efficiency. Non-cycling refrigerated air dryers run continuously, providing a consistent dew point, but consuming more energy. Both types are essential for maintaining the quality of compressed air in different applications.</p></p>
