<p><ul><li>Single Bucket</li><li>Continuous Bucket</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface mining equipment can be divided into two main types: single bucket and continuous bucket. Single bucket equipment includes front-end loaders and hydraulic shovels that require manual loading and dumping of material. Continuous bucket equipment, on the other hand, includes draglines and bucket-wheel excavators that are able to continuously remove and dump material without the need for manual intervention. Both types of equipment play a crucial role in surface mining operations by efficiently extracting resources from the earth.</p></p>
