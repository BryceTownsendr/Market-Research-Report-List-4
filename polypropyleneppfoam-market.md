<p><p>Polypropylene (PP) foam is commonly used in three types of markets: Extrusion Molding, Injection Foaming Molding, and Compression Molding. </p><p>Extrusion Molding involves forcing molten PP foam through a die to create various shapes and sizes. </p><p>Injection Foaming Molding uses a similar process, but the foam is injected into a mold under high pressure to produce intricate shapes and designs. </p><p>Compression Molding involves placing the PP foam into a mold and applying heat and pressure to form the desired product. Each market type offers unique advantages and applications for PP foam products.</p></p>
