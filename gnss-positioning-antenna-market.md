<p><ul><li>Patch Antennas</li><li>Helical Antennas</li><li>Choke-ring Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Patch antennas are small, low-profile antennas commonly used in GNSS positioning systems. They provide high gain and excellent radiation characteristics. Helical antennas are compact, circularly polarized antennas suitable for GNSS applications requiring high precision. Choke-ring antennas are designed to minimize multipath interference in GNSS receivers by reducing signals from low elevation angles. Each type of antenna offers unique advantages for GNSS positioning, catering to various needs and environments in the market.</p></p>
