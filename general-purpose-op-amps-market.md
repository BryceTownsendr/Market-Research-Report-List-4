<p><strong>The General-purpose Op Amps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bipolar Type</li><li>CMOS Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>General-purpose op amps are commonly categorized into two main types: bipolar and CMOS. Bipolar op amps are known for their higher input impedance, lower input current noise, and faster response times. They are suitable for applications that require precision and high-speed operation. On the other hand, CMOS op amps offer lower power consumption, wider operating voltage range, and better tolerance to input offset voltage. They are ideal for battery-operated devices and low-power applications. Both types cater to a wide range of general-purpose applications in various industries.</p></p>
