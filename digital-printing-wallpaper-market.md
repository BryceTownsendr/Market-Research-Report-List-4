<p><strong>The Digital Printing Wallpaper Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inkjet</li><li>Electrophotography</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital printing wallpaper market consists of two main types, inkjet and electrophotography. </p><p>Inkjet printing involves spraying ink onto the wallpaper surface to create vibrant and detailed designs. This method is popular for its high quality and versatility, allowing for customization and personalization.</p><p>Electrophotography, also known as digital toner printing, uses a dry toner to transfer images onto wallpaper. This method is known for its speed and efficiency, making it a popular choice for high volume printing. Both technologies offer unique benefits for different market needs.</p></p>
