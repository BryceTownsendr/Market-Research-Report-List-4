<p><p>Legionella testing market includes various types such as Microbial Culture, DFA Stain, PCR, and Others. Microbial culture involves growing Legionella bacteria in a laboratory setting. DFA stain is a technique that uses fluorescent dyes to detect Legionella cells. PCR (polymerase chain reaction) is a molecular method for detecting Legionella DNA. Other types of testing may include immunological methods or rapid antigen tests. Each method has different sensitivities, specificities, and turnaround times for Legionella detection.</p></p>
