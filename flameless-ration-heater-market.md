<p><p>Flameless ration heaters are commonly used in military and emergency situations to heat up meals without the need for an open flame. There are two main types of flameless ration heaters on the market: water heating and air heating. Water heating technology involves activating a chemical reaction that releases heat, which in turn heats up water surrounding the food pouch. Air heating technology involves a similar chemical reaction that heats up the air inside a pouch, providing a source of heat to warm up the meal.</p></p>
