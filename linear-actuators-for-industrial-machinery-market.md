<p><p>Linear actuators are essential components in industrial machinery, providing motion and control in various applications. They come in three main types: pneumatic, electric, and hydraulic. Pneumatic linear actuators use compressed air to generate linear motion, making them suitable for quick and precise movements. Electric linear actuators are powered by electricity, offering efficient and reliable performance. Hydraulic linear actuators rely on hydraulic fluids to produce motion, ideal for heavy-duty applications requiring high force output. Each type has its unique characteristics and advantages for different industrial needs.</p></p>
