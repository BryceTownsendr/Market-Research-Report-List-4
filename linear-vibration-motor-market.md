<p><strong>The Linear Vibration Motor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Moving-coil Type</li><li>Moving-iron Type</li><li>Moving-magnet Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Linear vibration motors are categorized into three main types based on their internal components and working mechanism. The moving-coil type operates by moving a coil within a magnetic field, creating vibrations. The moving-iron type features a stationary coil and a moving iron mass that generates vibrations. The moving-magnet type consists of a stationary magnet and a moving coil, which produces vibrations when the coil moves. Each type has its unique advantages and applications in various industries such as consumer electronics, automotive, and medical devices.</p></p>
