<p><p>Compounding Aseptic Containment Isolator (CACI) markets can be classified into two types: Recirculating and Total Exhaust. Recirculating isolators have a closed-loop design that recycles air within the isolator, while Total Exhaust isolators continuously exhaust air from the isolator to maintain a clean environment. Each type has its own benefits and applications, with recirculating isolators being more energy-efficient and total exhaust isolators providing better containment of hazardous materials. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining aseptic conditions in pharmaceutical compounding processes.</p></p>
