<p><strong>The Padmount Transformers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dry-type Padmount Transformers</li><li>Liquid-immersed Padmount Transformers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Padmount transformers are available in two main types: dry-type and liquid-immersed. Dry-type padmount transformers use air or a non-flammable gas to cool the transformer, making them safer for indoor installations. These transformers are commonly used in residential and commercial buildings where fire safety is a concern. In contrast, liquid-immersed padmount transformers use mineral oil or silicone oil as a coolant, making them suitable for outdoor installations and heavy-duty industrial applications where higher power levels are required.</p></p>
