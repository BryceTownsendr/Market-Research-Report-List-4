<p><p>The hair coloring product market is divided into three main types - Temporary Hair Dye, Semi-Permanent Hair Dye, and Permanent Hair Dye. Temporary hair dye is formulated to last for a short period and can be easily washed out. Semi-permanent hair dye lasts longer than temporary dye but eventually fades over time. Permanent hair dye, as the name suggests, permanently changes the color of the hair until it grows out. Each type offers different levels of longevity and commitment to a new hair color.</p></p>
