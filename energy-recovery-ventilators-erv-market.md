<p><p>Energy Recovery Ventilators (ERV) are devices used to improve indoor air quality by exchanging stale indoor air with fresh outdoor air while also recovering energy from the outgoing air. There are three main types of ERVs in the market - rotary enthalpy wheel, fixed plate, and others. Rotary enthalpy wheels are rotating heat exchangers that transfer both heat and moisture between the incoming and outgoing air streams. Fixed plate ERVs use stationary plates to transfer heat only. Other types of ERVs include heat pipes, run-around coils, and porous membranes.</p></p>
