<p><strong>The Electronic Ceramics Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Ferroelectric</li><li>Piezoelectric</li><li>Pyroelectric</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Electronic Ceramics Market includes three major types: Ferroelectric, Piezoelectric, and Pyroelectric ceramics. </p><p>Ferroelectric ceramics have a spontaneous electric polarization that can be reversed by an external electric field, making them ideal for applications such as capacitors and sensors. </p><p>Piezoelectric ceramics generate an electric charge in response to mechanical stress, commonly used in transducers and actuators. </p><p>Pyroelectric ceramics produce an electric charge in response to changes in temperature, suitable for applications like infrared detectors. These types play crucial roles in various electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
