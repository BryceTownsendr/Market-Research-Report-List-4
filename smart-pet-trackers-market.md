<p><p>Smart pet trackers come in various market types, including GPS based trackers, radio based trackers, and others. GPS based trackers use satellite technology to pinpoint the location of the pet in real-time, allowing owners to track their pet's movements accurately. Radio based trackers rely on radio frequency signals emitted by a collar attached to the pet, which can be picked up by a receiver in order to locate the pet. Other types of trackers may use different technologies like Bluetooth or cellular networks to track pets.</p></p>
