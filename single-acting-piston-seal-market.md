<p><p>Single acting piston seals can be classified into two types: symmetric and asymmetric. Symmetrical piston seals have the same design and perform equally in both directions of motion. They are suitable for applications where the direction of movement is not predetermined. On the other hand, asymmetric piston seals have a specific design that provides better performance in one direction compared to the other. They are ideal for applications that require optimized sealing performance in a specific direction.</p></p>
