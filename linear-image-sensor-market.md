<p><p>Linear image sensor market is dominated by three types: CMOS, CCD, and others. CMOS sensors are known for their low power consumption and cost-effective production, making them a popular choice for consumer electronics. On the other hand, CCD sensors offer higher image quality and light sensitivity, making them preferred for scientific and industrial applications. The "others" category includes niche sensor technologies like hybrid sensors and vertical overflow devices. Each type has its own strengths and is tailored to different market needs.</p></p>
