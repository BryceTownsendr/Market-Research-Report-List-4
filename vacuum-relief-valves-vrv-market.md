<p><p>Vacuum Relief Valves (VRVs) are classified into different types based on their operating mechanisms. The most common types are the Spring-Loaded Type and Weight-Loaded Type. Spring-Loaded VRVs use a spring to control the opening and closing of the valve in response to changes in pressure. Weight-Loaded VRVs, on the other hand, use a weighted lever mechanism to achieve the same purpose. Other types of VRVs may include pilot-operated valves or diaphragm-operated valves, each offering unique features and benefits for specific applications.</p></p>
