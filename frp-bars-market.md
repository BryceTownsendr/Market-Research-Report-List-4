<p><p>FRP bars market consists of three main types: GFRP bars, CFRP bars, and others. GFRP bars, also known as glass fiber reinforced polymer bars, are made of glass fibers and resin and are corrosion-resistant, making them ideal for concrete reinforcement. CFRP bars, or carbon fiber reinforced polymer bars, are made of carbon fibers and resin and offer high strength and stiffness. The "Others" segment includes other types of FRP bars such as aramid fiber reinforced polymer bars.</p></p>
