<p><p>Membrane dryers are used for removing moisture from compressed air in various industries. Two types of membrane dryers are porous membrane dryers and non-porous membrane dryers. Porous membrane dryers utilize a membrane material that allows only water vapor to pass through, while non-porous membrane dryers use a selective membrane that separates water molecules from the air. Both types are efficient in removing moisture without the need for external power sources, making them popular choices for applications where traditional drying methods are not suitable.</p></p>
