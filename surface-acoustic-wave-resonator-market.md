<p><p>Surface Acoustic Wave Resonators are used in various electronic devices for signal processing and filtering applications. There are two main types of markets for these resonators: DIP (Dual In-line Package) and Surface Mount. DIP resonators are traditionally used in older devices and are soldered onto circuit boards. Surface Mount resonators are more commonly used in modern devices and are mounted directly onto the surface of circuit boards using surface mount technology. Both types of resonators serve the same purpose of providing precise frequency control in electronic systems.</p></p>
