<p><p>Speed reducers are mechanical devices used to decrease the speed of a motor while increasing its torque output. The main types of speed reducers include gear reducers, which use gears to reduce speed and increase torque; worm wheel reducers, which utilize a worm gear to achieve speed reduction; planetary gears, which consist of multiple gears working together to reduce speed; cycloid reducers, which use a cycloidal disc to slow down the motor; and harmonic drives, which employ flexible components to achieve speed reduction with high precision.</p></p>
