Peritoneal Dialysis uses the lining of the abdomen to filter waste from the blood. Kidney Transplantation involves surgically transplanting a healthy kidney from a donor into a patient with ESRD. These treatments provide essential renal function for individuals with advanced kidney disease.</p></p>
