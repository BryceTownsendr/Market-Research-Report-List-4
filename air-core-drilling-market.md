<p><p>Air core drilling is a prevalent method in the mining and exploration industry, utilizing air as the drilling fluid. Different market types within air core drilling include dust drilling, mist drilling, foam drilling, aerated fluid drilling, and nitrogen membrane drilling. Dust drilling involves the use of dry air to carry cuttings to the surface. Mist drilling uses a mist of water to create a slurry for cuttings removal. Foam drilling involves creating foam to lift cuttings. Aerated fluid drilling mixes air with water for cuttings removal. Nitrogen membrane drilling uses nitrogen gas for hole stabilization.</p></p>
