<p><ul><li>Lateral Flow</li><li>Agglutination Assays</li><li>Flow-Through</li><li>Biosensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rapid medical diagnostic devices market encompasses various types of technologies such as lateral flow, agglutination assays, flow-through, and biosensors. Lateral flow devices are simple paper-based tests that provide quick results for diseases like pregnancy and infectious diseases. Agglutination assays detect the presence of antibodies or antigens through the clumping of particles. Flow-through devices utilize capillary action for analysis. Biosensors involve biological components like enzymes or antibodies to detect specific targets. These technologies cater to the growing demand for quick and efficient diagnostic solutions.</p></p>
