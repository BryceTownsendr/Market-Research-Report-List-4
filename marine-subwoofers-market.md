<p><p>Marine subwoofers come in two main types: powered subwoofers and passive subwoofers. Powered subwoofers have a built-in amplifier, making them easier to install and typically providing more power. They are ideal for those looking for a simple plug-and-play solution. On the other hand, passive subwoofers require an external amplifier to provide power. They are often favored by those looking for a more customizable setup and greater control over sound output. Both types cater to different needs and preferences in the marine audio market.</p></p>
