<p><strong>The Fuel Cell DC-DC Converter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Isolated DC-DC Converter</li><li>Non-Isolated DC-DC Converter</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fuel cell DC-DC converters can be classified into two main types: isolated and non-isolated converters. Isolated DC-DC converters provide electrical isolation between the input and output, ensuring safety and reducing noise. They are commonly used in applications where isolation is crucial, such as medical devices and industrial equipment. On the other hand, non-isolated converters do not provide isolation and are more cost-effective and simpler in design. They are often used in less sensitive applications such as automotive and consumer electronics.</p></p>
