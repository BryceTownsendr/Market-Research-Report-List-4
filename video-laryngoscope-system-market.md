<p><p>Video laryngoscopes are medical devices used for intubation in difficult airway situations. There are two main types of video laryngoscopes: un-channelled and channelled. Un-channelled video laryngoscopes have a straight blade design, providing a clear view of the vocal cords. Channelled video laryngoscopes have a built-in channel allowing for easier insertion of the endotracheal tube. Both types are essential tools for medical professionals in ensuring successful intubation in a variety of clinical settings.</p></p>
