<p><p>Seismic isolators for buildings are categorized into different market types based on their design and functionality. These market types include Natural Rubber Bearings (NRB), Lead Rubber Bearings (LRB), High Damping Rubber Bearings (HDR), and others. NRB isolators use natural rubber to absorb seismic energy, while LRB isolators use lead cores for damping. HDR isolators have higher damping properties for enhanced seismic protection. Other types include sliding isolators and elastomeric bearings with different features and applications in seismic isolation technology for buildings.</p></p>
