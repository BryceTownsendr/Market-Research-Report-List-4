<p><p>The basal thermometer market consists of two main types: electronic thermometers and infrared thermometers. Electronic thermometers are more traditional devices that measure temperature internally through the mouth, armpit, or rectum. Infrared thermometers, on the other hand, are contactless devices that measure temperature by sensing the heat radiated by the body. Both types of thermometers are commonly used in tracking basal body temperature for fertility tracking or monitoring ovulation. Each type has its own advantages and may be preferred by different individuals based on their needs and preferences.</p></p>
