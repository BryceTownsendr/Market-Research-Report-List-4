<p><p>Calibration baths are used for calibrating temperature devices and sensors. There are two main types of calibration baths: dry-block baths and liquid baths. Dry-block baths use a metal block with holes to hold temperature sensors, while liquid baths use a liquid medium to calibrate sensors. Dry-block baths are more portable and quicker to reach temperature, while liquid baths offer better uniformity. Both types are commonly used in industries such as pharmaceuticals, food processing, and manufacturing for accurate temperature calibration.</p></p>
