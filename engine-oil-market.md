<p><p>Engine oil market can be divided into two main types: mineral oil and synthetic oil. Mineral oil is derived from crude oil and has been used for many years. It is known for its affordability and basic lubrication properties. On the other hand, synthetic oil is man-made and offers superior performance and protection for engines. Although synthetic oil is more expensive, it provides better engine cleanliness, improved fuel economy, and longer-lasting performance compared to mineral oil. Both types cater to different customer needs and preferences in the automotive industry.</p></p>
