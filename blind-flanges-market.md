<p><ul><li>FF</li><li>RF</li><li>MFM</li><li>TG</li><li>RJ</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Blind flanges are available in various types to suit different piping systems. FF (Flat Face) flanges have a flat surface for easy alignment, while RF (Raised Face) flanges have a raised ring to create a seal. MFM (Male Female) flanges have a protruding male end and a recessed female end for easy connection. TG (Tongue and Groove) flanges have a tongue and groove design for added stability. RJ (Ring Joint) flanges are designed to withstand high pressure and temperature applications with a ring joint gasket.</p></p>
