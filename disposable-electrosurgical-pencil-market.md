<p><p>Disposable electrosurgical pencils are available in two main types - hand control and foot control. Hand control pencils are operated manually by the surgeon through buttons on the pencil itself, allowing for precise control during procedures. Foot control pencils, on the other hand, are operated by the surgeon using a foot pedal, providing hands-free operation and freeing up the surgeon's hands for other tasks. Both types offer different levels of convenience and control, catering to the preferences and needs of surgeons during electrosurgical procedures.</p></p>
