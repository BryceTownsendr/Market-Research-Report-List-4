<p><p>Full dentures are a type of dental prosthesis that replace all of the teeth in a patient's mouth. They are removable and are typically made of acrylic or other materials that mimic the appearance and function of natural teeth. Full dentures are commonly used by people who have lost all of their natural teeth due to decay, trauma, or other reasons.</p><p>The Full Dentures Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 5.10% during the forecast period. This growth can be attributed to several factors, including the increasing prevalence of dental problems among the global population, the growing awareness about oral health, and advancements in dental technology. Additionally, the rising geriatric population, who are more prone to tooth loss, is driving the demand for full dentures.</p><p>Some of the latest trends in the Full Dentures Market include the growing adoption of digital dentistry, which allows for more precise and customized dentures, as well as the increasing use of CAD/CAM technology in the fabrication of dentures. Moreover, there is a growing focus on the aesthetics of dentures, with dentists and patients alike seeking natural-looking and comfortable prostheses. Overall, the Full Dentures Market is expected to continue growing in the coming years as the demand for dental prostheses remains strong.</p></p>
