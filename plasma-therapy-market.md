<p><p>Plasma therapy market consists of various types of treatments derived from the blood plasma of patients. Pure PRP involves extracting and injecting Platelet-rich Plasma, which promotes tissue repair and regeneration. Leucocyte-rich PRP contains white blood cells along with platelets, enhancing the immune response and healing process. Leukocyte-rich Fibrin (L-PRF) involves the conversion of platelet concentrate into a fibrin matrix, which aids in wound healing and tissue regeneration. These different types of plasma therapy cater to a wide range of medical needs and conditions.</p></p>
