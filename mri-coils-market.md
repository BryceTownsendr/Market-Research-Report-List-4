<p><p>MRI Coil Market: Phased-array and Quadrature coils are two common types used in MRI imaging. Phased-array coils consist of multiple elements working together to improve signal-to-noise ratio and image quality. They are flexible and can be adjusted for different patient sizes. Quadrature coils use two opposing coils to produce a higher quality image than single coils. They offer better sensitivity and coverage in imaging. Both types play a crucial role in enhancing the performance and precision of MRI machines in various medical settings.</p></p>
