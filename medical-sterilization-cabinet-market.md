<p><p>Medical sterilization cabinets are used in healthcare facilities to safely sterilize medical instruments and equipment. There are two main types of medical sterilization cabinets: bench-top and vertical. Bench-top cabinets are smaller units that can be placed on a counter or tabletop, while vertical cabinets are larger units that stand upright on the floor. Both types use heat, steam, and sometimes chemicals to ensure that all items inside are thoroughly sterilized and free of harmful bacteria or pathogens.</p></p>
