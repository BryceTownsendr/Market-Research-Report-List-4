<p><p>Internal circulation ball screws are commonly categorized into two main types: single nut and double nut. Single nut ball screws have only one nut attached to the screw shaft, making them suitable for applications with low to moderate load requirements. Double nut ball screws, on the other hand, have two nuts on the shaft, providing greater load capacity and improved rigidity. Double nut ball screws are preferred in applications where high precision and heavy loads are necessary. Both types play a crucial role in various industrial sectors such as aerospace, automotive, and manufacturing.</p></p>
