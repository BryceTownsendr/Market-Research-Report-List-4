<p><p>Steam conditioning valves are critical components used in steam systems to regulate the flow and pressure of steam. The market for these valves can be segmented into three main types: angle-style valves, globe-style valves, and other types. Angle-style valves are commonly used for high-pressure applications and can provide precise control over steam flow. Globe-style valves are ideal for moderate-pressure applications and offer good throttling capabilities. Other types of steam conditioning valves include piston-style valves, ball valves, and butterfly valves, each suited for specific steam system requirements.</p></p>
