<p><p>Disposable dental articulators are used in dentistry to hold models of a patient's teeth during procedures. There are three main types of disposable dental articulators - flat type, slot type, and oblique type. Flat type articulators have a flat surface for easy mounting of models. Slot type articulators have slots for securing models in place. Oblique type articulators have an angled design for better accessibility during procedures. Each type offers unique benefits and is chosen based on the specific needs of the dentist and patient.</p></p>
