<p><ul><li>Continuous Level Monitoring</li><li>Point Level Monitoring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical level sensors are used for continuous level monitoring and point level monitoring in various industries. Continuous level monitoring involves real-time measurement of the liquid level in a tank or container. This type of sensor provides a continuous output signal to indicate the exact level. Point level monitoring, on the other hand, is used to detect a specific level at a predetermined point, triggering an alarm or control action when the level reaches a certain threshold. Both types of sensors are crucial for ensuring efficient and safe operations in industrial processes.</p></p>
