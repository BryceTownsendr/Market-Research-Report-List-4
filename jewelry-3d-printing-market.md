<p><p>Jewelry 3D Printing market offers various types of technologies, primarily including Stereolithography (SLA), Selective Laser Sintering (SLS), Digital Light Processing (DLP), and Fused Depositing Modelling (FDM). SLA uses a UV laser to cure layers of resin, while SLS involves using a laser to sinter powdered material together. DLP creates objects by projecting light patterns onto a liquid resin. FDM works by heating and extruding thermoplastic filament. Other types of technologies in this market include Binder jetting and Material Jetting.</p></p>
