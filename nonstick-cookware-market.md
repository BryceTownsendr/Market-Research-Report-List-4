<p><strong>The Nonstick Cookware Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PTFE-based Nonstick Cookware</li><li>Ceramic-based Nonstick Cookware</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PTFE-based nonstick cookware is made with a synthetic chemical coating that prevents food from sticking to the pan. It is known for its durability and scratch resistance. On the other hand, ceramic-based nonstick cookware is made with a ceramic coating that is free of PTFE and PFOA, making it a more environmentally friendly option. Ceramic cookware is also non-reactive and provides even heat distribution. Both types of nonstick cookware are popular choices in the market for their convenience and ease of use.</p></p>
