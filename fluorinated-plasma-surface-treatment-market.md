<p><p>Fluorinated plasma surface treatment is a method used to modify the surface properties of materials by introducing fluorine atoms onto the surface. This treatment can be done using low-pressure plasma or atmospheric plasma. Low-pressure plasma treatment is carried out in a vacuum chamber, while atmospheric plasma treatment is done in open air. Both methods offer different advantages depending on the specific application requirements, with low-pressure plasma often being used for more precise control and atmospheric plasma for faster processing.</p></p>
