Hydraulic Advance system utilizes hydraulic pressure to provide precision support for workpieces during machining operations. This type of system offers high accuracy and stability in various industrial applications. On the other hand, the Spring Advance system relies on mechanical springs to provide support for workpieces. This type of system is cost-effective and suitable for lighter workloads where lower precision is acceptable. Both systems play a crucial role in enhancing productivity and efficiency in manufacturing processes.</p></p>
