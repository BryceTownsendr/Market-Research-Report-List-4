<p><p>Ultrasound imaging market types include 2D imaging, which shows two-dimensional images of the internal structures of the body; 3D imaging, which provides three-dimensional images for a more detailed view; and 4D imaging, which adds a fourth dimension of real-time movement to the image. Doppler imaging is used to assess blood flow and detect any abnormalities. Other types of ultrasound imaging may include contrast-enhanced imaging or elastography, which can provide additional information about tissue characteristics.</p></p>
