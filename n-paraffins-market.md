<p><ul><li>Light Type</li><li>Heavy Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>N-Paraffins can be categorized into two main types in the market: Light Type and Heavy Type. Light Type n-Paraffins have lower carbon chain lengths and are usually more volatile and easily evaporate at room temperature. They are commonly used in industries such as solvents, fuel additives, and lubricants. On the other hand, Heavy Type n-Paraffins have higher carbon chain lengths and are more viscous and stable, making them suitable for applications like wax production, manufacturing of surfactants, and as raw materials in plastics and rubber production.</p></p>
