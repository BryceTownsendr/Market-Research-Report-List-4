<p><ul><li>Thermal Black</li><li>Furnace Black</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Carbon blacks for rubber can be categorized into two main types: Thermal Black and Furnace Black. Thermal Black is produced by the partial combustion of hydrocarbons at high temperatures, resulting in a fine particle size and high structure, making it ideal for applications requiring high reinforcement properties. On the other hand, Furnace Black is produced by the incomplete combustion of hydrocarbons at lower temperatures, resulting in larger particle sizes and lower structure, making it suitable for applications requiring better abrasion resistance and color properties.</p></p>
