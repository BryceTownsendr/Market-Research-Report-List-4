<p><p>A Ball Bonder is a machine used in the semiconductor industry for wire bonding, a process that creates electrical connections between a semiconductor device and its package. Manufacturers are focusing on enhancing the speed, precision, and reliability of ball bonding machines to meet the evolving requirements of semiconductor manufacturers.
