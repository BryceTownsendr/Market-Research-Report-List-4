<p><p>Hydrogen generators for power plants come in two main types: Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzers and PEM Electrolyzers. Traditional Alkaline Electrolyzers are the most common type, using potassium hydroxide as the electrolyte. They are cost-effective and have been used for many years. On the other hand, PEM Electrolyzers use a proton exchange membrane as an electrolyte, making them more efficient and compact. They are gaining popularity in the market due to their higher efficiency and ability to operate at higher pressures.</p></p>
