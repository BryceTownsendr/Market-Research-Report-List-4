<p><p>Oil mist separators are devices used to remove oil mist from the air in industrial applications to maintain a clean and safe working environment. Mounted oil mist separators are installed directly on the machine generating the mist, while stand oil mist separators are standalone units positioned in close proximity to the source. Mounted separators are ideal for space-constrained areas, while stand separators offer more flexibility in placement. Both types effectively capture and remove oil mist particles, helping to improve air quality and protect machinery.</p></p>
