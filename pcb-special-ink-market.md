<p><ul><li>Heat Curing Ink</li><li>UV Curable Ink</li><li>Photosensitive Ink</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The PCB Special Ink market is segmented into three main types: Heat Curing Ink, UV Curable Ink, and Photosensitive Ink. Heat Curing Ink is cured by applying heat, making it ideal for high-temperature applications. UV Curable Ink cures instantly when exposed to ultraviolet light, providing fast and efficient processing. Photosensitive Ink uses light-sensitive materials that harden when exposed to specific wavelengths of light, offering precise and intricate designs. Each type of ink caters to different requirements in the PCB industry, providing diverse options for manufacturers.</p></p>
