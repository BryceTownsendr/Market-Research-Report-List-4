<p><p>Monocolumn stacker cranes and two-column stacker cranes are two types of robot stacker crane markets. Monocolumn stacker cranes consist of a single column that moves horizontally and vertically to retrieve and stack materials. They are typically used in smaller warehouses or storage facilities. Two-column stacker cranes, on the other hand, have two vertical columns that work together to lift and transport heavier loads. They are commonly found in larger industrial settings where heavy materials need to be handled efficiently.</p></p>
