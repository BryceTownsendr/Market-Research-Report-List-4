<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Steam Superheater Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radiant Superheaters</li><li>Convection Superheaters</li><li>Combined Superheaters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Steam superheaters are classified into three types: radiant superheaters, convection superheaters, and combined superheaters. Radiant superheaters are located in the combustion chamber and are exposed to high temperatures, while convection superheaters are placed in the flue gas path to absorb heat. Combined superheaters incorporate both radiant and convection elements to maximize steam temperature and efficiency. Each type of superheater plays a crucial role in enhancing the performance of steam boilers and industrial processes by ensuring the steam is superheated to the desired temperature.</p></p>
