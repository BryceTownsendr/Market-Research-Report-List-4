Low-voltage cables are designed for use in wind turbines with voltage requirements below 1 kV, while medium-voltage cables are suitable for turbines with voltage requirements ranging from 1 kV to 69 kV. The distinction between the two types of cables is important for ensuring that wind turbines are equipped with the appropriate cables to optimize their performance and efficiency.</p></p>
