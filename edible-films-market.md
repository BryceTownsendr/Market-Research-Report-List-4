<p><p>Edible films market is categorized into various types based on the materials used for their production. Protein-based films are made from proteins like gelatin or soy protein and are widely used for encapsulating food ingredients. Polysaccharides films are made from carbohydrates like cellulose or starch, providing a barrier against moisture and oxygen. Lipids-based films are derived from fats like waxes or oils, offering good barrier properties. Other types of edible films may include combinations of different materials for specific applications in the food industry.</p></p>
