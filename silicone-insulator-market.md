<p><p>Silicone insulators are commonly categorized into three main types based on their applications in the market. Post insulators are primarily used to support overhead high voltage transmission lines. Suspension insulators are designed to hang power lines from towers. Other types of silicone insulators include pin insulators, shackle insulators, and long rod insulators, which are used in various electrical systems for insulation purposes. Each type of insulator plays a crucial role in maintaining the integrity and efficiency of electrical power distribution systems.</p></p>
