<p><p>There are various types of business printer markets based on the printing technology they use. Lithographic printers use a plate-based printing process, while digital printers utilize digital files for printing. Flexographic printers use flexible plates for printing on various substrates. Screen printers use a mesh stencil to transfer ink onto the printing surface. Gravure printers use a recessed image on a cylinder to transfer ink. Other types of printers include letterpress, offset, and 3D printers, each with their unique printing methods.</p></p>
