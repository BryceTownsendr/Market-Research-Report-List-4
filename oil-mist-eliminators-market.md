<p><p>Oil Mist Eliminators are used to remove oil mist from the air and prevent environmental pollution. Two popular types of Oil Mist Eliminators in the market are Electrostatic Filtration and Frame Filter. </p><p>Electrostatic Filtration uses an electric charge to attract and collect oil mist particles, effectively removing them from the air. </p><p>On the other hand, Frame Filter works by passing the air through a filter made of fibers or mesh, trapping the oil mist particles and allowing clean air to pass through. Both types are widely used in industrial settings to maintain air quality and reduce emissions.</p></p>
