<p><p>Micro milling machines are classified into three main types based on their axis configurations: 3-axis, 5-axis, and others. 3-axis machines are the most common, with movement along the X, Y, and Z axes. 5-axis machines add rotation around two additional axes, allowing for more complex and precise cuts. The "others" category includes machines with unique axis configurations, such as 4-axis machines. Each type offers different capabilities and is chosen based on the specific requirements of the milling task.</p></p>
