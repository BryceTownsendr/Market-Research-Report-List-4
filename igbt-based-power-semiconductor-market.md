<p><p>IGBT-based power semiconductors are available in two main market types: discrete and module. Discrete IGBTs are standalone components that are sold individually, providing flexibility in design and customization. Module IGBTs, on the other hand, consist of several IGBTs and other components packaged together, offering convenience and ease of installation. Discrete IGBTs are commonly used in smaller-scale applications where customization is important, while module IGBTs are preferred for larger scale applications that require higher power output and reliability.</p></p>
