<p><p>CIP (clean-in-place) cleaning systems are used in various industries to clean equipment and pipelines without disassembling them. There are two main types of CIP cleaning systems in the market: manual type and automatic type. Manual CIP systems require operators to physically interact with the equipment to clean it, while automatic CIP systems operate with minimal human intervention, using sensors and programmable logic controllers to clean equipment. Both types of systems are vital in industries such as food and beverage, pharmaceuticals, and dairy.</p></p>
