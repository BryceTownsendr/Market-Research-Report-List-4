<p><p>Copper foil market can be categorized into two main types: Rolled Copper Foil and Electrolytic Copper Foil. Rolled Copper Foil is produced by rolling a piece of copper into a thin sheet, making it flexible and ideal for use in printed circuit boards and lithium-ion batteries. On the other hand, Electrolytic Copper Foil is created through an electroplating process, resulting in a smoother and more uniform surface, making it suitable for high-frequency circuit boards and electromagnetic shielding applications.</p></p>
