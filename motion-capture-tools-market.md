<p><p>Motion capture tools market can be categorized into three main types: Optical 3D Motion Capture Systems, Inertial 3D Motion Capture Systems, and Others. Optical systems use cameras and markers to track movement in a 3D space, providing high accuracy but requiring a controlled environment. Inertial systems use sensors to capture movement without the need for a fixed space, making them more portable and versatile. Other types may include magnetic systems or hybrid systems combining different technologies for specific purposes in the motion capture industry.</p></p>
