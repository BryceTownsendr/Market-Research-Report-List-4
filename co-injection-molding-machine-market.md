<p><p>Co-injection molding machines are available in three main types: manual, semi-automatic, and automatic. Manual machines require more operator involvement and control throughout the molding process. Semi-automatic machines offer some automated features but still require manual intervention at certain stages. Automatic machines are completely automated, with minimal need for operator intervention. Each type caters to different levels of production needs, with manual machines being suitable for small-scale operations, semi-automatic machines for medium-scale production, and automatic machines for high-volume manufacturing.</p></p>
