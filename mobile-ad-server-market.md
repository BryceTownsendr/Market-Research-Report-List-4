<p><p>Mobile ad servers can be classified into two main types: First Party and Third Party Ad Servers. First party ad servers are owned and operated by the advertiser or publisher, giving them more control over ad placement and targeting. Third party ad servers, on the other hand, are independent platforms that serve ads across multiple websites. Additionally, ad servers can be further classified as Hosted or Self-Hosted, based on whether the server infrastructure is managed by a third-party provider or maintained in-house by the advertiser or publisher.</p></p>
