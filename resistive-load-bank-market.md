<p><p>The resistive load bank market comprises of three main types: Portable load banks, trailer mounted load banks, and stationary load banks. Portable load banks are lightweight and easily transportable, making them ideal for testing in different locations. Trailer mounted load banks are mobile units that can be towed behind a vehicle, providing flexibility in testing applications. Stationary load banks are fixed in one location and are typically larger in size, offering higher power capacities for continuous testing and monitoring of electrical systems.</p></p>
