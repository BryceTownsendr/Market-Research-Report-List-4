<p><p>Vacutainer market types include Serum Separating Tubes, EDTA Tubes, Plasma Separation Tubes, and others. Serum Separating Tubes are used for separating blood serum from clotting factors. EDTA Tubes contain the anticoagulant EDTA to prevent blood clotting. Plasma Separation Tubes have gel separators for isolating plasma from blood cells. Other types may include heparin tubes or citrate tubes. Each type is designed for specific blood tests or procedures, catering to the diverse needs of healthcare professionals and patients.</p></p>
