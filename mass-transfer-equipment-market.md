<p><p>Mass transfer equipment market consists of various types such as Column Internals, Random Packing, Structured Packing, and Trays. Column Internals are components placed inside columns to enhance mass transfer efficiency. Random Packing involves randomly placed packing material to increase surface area for mass transfer. Structured Packing consists of well-organized packing material to promote efficient mass transfer. Trays are horizontal plates with holes for vapor and liquid flow. Each type serves a specific purpose in the chemical and process industries to facilitate mass transfer operations.</p></p>
