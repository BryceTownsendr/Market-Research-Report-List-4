<p><strong>The Jacketed Pressure Vessels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Conventional Vessel Jackets</li><li>Dimple Vessel Jackets</li><li>Half Pipe Coil Vessel Jackets</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Jacketed pressure vessels come in three main types: conventional vessel jackets, dimple vessel jackets, and half pipe coil vessel jackets. Conventional vessel jackets are a simple design with a full external jacket for heating or cooling. Dimple vessel jackets have dimpled sheets welded to the vessel for better heat transfer. Half pipe coil vessel jackets have half pipes welded to the vessel that allow for higher pressure and temperature applications. Each type offers unique advantages for different industrial applications.</p></p>
