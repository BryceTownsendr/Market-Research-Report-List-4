<p><p>The Industrial Equipment Fastener market can be categorized into two main types: Threaded Fasteners and Non-Threaded Fasteners. Threaded fasteners include screws, bolts, and nuts that have a spiral ridge pattern, allowing for easy installation and removal. Non-threaded fasteners, on the other hand, include pins, rivets, and clips that do not have spiral ridges but instead use other methods such as friction to secure components together. Both types of fasteners play a crucial role in the assembly and maintenance of industrial equipment.</p></p>
