<p><p>Bus duct market types include BMC (Air Splicing Bus Duct), CMC (Intensive Insulation Plug Bus Duct), and CFW (Higher Strength Enclosed Bus Duct). BMC uses air splicing technology for easy installation and maintenance. CMC features intensive insulation plug for enhanced safety and efficiency. CFW offers higher strength with enclosed design for durability and reliability. Each type caters to different needs in various industries, providing versatile solutions for electrical distribution systems.</p></p>
