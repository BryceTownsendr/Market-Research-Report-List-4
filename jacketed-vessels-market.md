<p><ul><li>Conventional Jackets</li><li>Half-Pipe Coil Jackets</li><li>Dimple Jackets</li><li>Plate Coils</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Jacketed vessels market types include conventional jackets, half-pipe coil jackets, dimple jackets, and plate coils. Conventional jackets provide a simple external covering for temperature regulation, while half-pipe coil jackets have coils inside the vessel for enhanced heat transfer. Dimple jackets have dimples on the outer surface for better temperature control. Plate coils consist of metal plates welded to the vessel for efficient heating or cooling. Each type offers unique benefits for various industrial applications requiring precise temperature control.</p></p>
