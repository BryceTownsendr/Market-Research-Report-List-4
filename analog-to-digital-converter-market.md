<p><p>Analog to Digital Converter (ADC) market consists of various types including Display ADC, Delta-sigma ADC, Pipelined ADC, and Dual Slope ADC. Display ADC focuses on accuracy and speed for visual display applications. Delta-sigma ADC offers high resolution and is commonly used in audio applications. Pipelined ADC divides the conversion process into stages for high-speed performance. Dual Slope ADC is known for its simplicity and reliability in measuring and converting analog signals. Each type caters to specific needs in various industries.</p></p>
