<p><p>Anti-static PCB brackets come in four main market types: L Shape, U Shape, H Shape, and Others. L Shape brackets have a design that resembles the letter "L" and provide support to PCBs at a right angle. U Shape brackets offer a rounded support to PCBs to prevent static electricity. H Shape brackets provide a reliable support system for PCBs with a design that resembles the letter "H". Other types of brackets may vary in shape and function, offering unique solutions for different PCB mounting needs.</p></p>
