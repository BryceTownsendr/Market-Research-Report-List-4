<p><strong>The Bragg Fiber Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hollow Core</li><li>Solid Core</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Bragg Fiber Market is classified into Hollow Core and Solid Core types based on their core structures. Hollow Core Bragg fibers have a hollow core running through the center, allowing light to travel through air or a low-index material. This design enables low-loss transmission over a wide range of wavelengths. On the other hand, Solid Core Bragg fibers have a solid core made of a high-index material, offering higher light confinement and better performance in certain applications. Both types have unique advantages and are utilized in various industrial and scientific applications.</p></p>
