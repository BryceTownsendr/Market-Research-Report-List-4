<p><strong>The Ventricular Assist Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>LVADs</li><li>RVADs</li><li>BIVADs</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ventricular Assist Devices (VADs) are mechanical pumps used to support heart function. Left Ventricular Assist Devices (LVADs) support the left side of the heart, Right Ventricular Assist Devices (RVADs) support the right side, and Biventricular Assist Devices (BiVADs) support both sides. LVADs are the most common type, providing support for patients with heart failure. RVADs are used when the right side of the heart is weakened, while BiVADs are used for patients with severe heart failure affecting both sides of the heart.</p></p>
