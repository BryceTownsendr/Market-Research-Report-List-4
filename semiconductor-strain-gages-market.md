<p><p>Semiconductor strain gauges are used to measure the deformation or strain of materials. The integrated semiconductor strain gauge is directly integrated into the material being measured, providing accurate and real-time data. Thin film semiconductor strain gauges are deposited onto the material's surface, offering high sensitivity and flexibility. Diffusion semiconductor strain gauges are created by diffusing impurities into the material, allowing for precise measurements. Epitaxial semiconductor strain gauges are grown on the material's surface, providing enhanced sensitivity and stability. Each type offers unique advantages for strain measurement applications.</p></p>
