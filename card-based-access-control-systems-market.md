<p><p>Card-based access control systems are divided into two main market types: card reader with button and card reader without button. Card reader with button systems require the user to input a pin or press a button in addition to swiping their access card, providing an extra layer of security. Both types offer convenient and secure access control options for various industries and applications.</p></p>
