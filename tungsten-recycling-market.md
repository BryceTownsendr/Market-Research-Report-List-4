<p><p>Tungsten recycling can be classified into two main types: direct methods and indirect methods. Indirect methods, on the other hand, involve the recovery of tungsten through secondary sources such as mining residues, electronic waste, and used tools and equipment. Both types of recycling methods play a crucial role in reducing the demand for primary tungsten resources and promoting sustainability in the tungsten industry.</p></p>
