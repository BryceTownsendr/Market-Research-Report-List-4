<p><p>In-Mold Electronic Inks are used for printing electronic circuits directly onto plastic surfaces during the injection molding process. There are three main types of inks used in this market: Silver Inks are used for conductive traces, Dielectric Inks are used for insulating layers, and Carbon-based Conductive Inks are used for electrodes and sensors. These different types of inks allow for the creation of complex electronic components within plastic parts, enabling the production of smart and interactive plastic products.</p></p>
