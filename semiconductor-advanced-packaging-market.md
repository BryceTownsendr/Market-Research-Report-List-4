<p><p>Semiconductor Advanced Packaging Market Types include Fan-Out Wafer-Level Packaging (FO WLP), which allows for more I/O connections and higher device performance. Fan-In Wafer-Level Packaging (FI WLP) provides a smaller form factor and better thermal performance. Flip Chip (FC) technology offers higher pin count and smaller package size. 2.5D/3D packaging enables stacking multiple dies in a single package, increasing device performance and reducing overall size. These different packaging types cater to various market needs, such as high performance, compact size, and cost efficiency.</p></p>
