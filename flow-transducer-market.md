<p><ul><li>Coriolis Flow Transducer</li><li>Mass Flow Transducer</li><li>Ultrasonic Flow Transducer</li><li>Vortex Flow Transducer</li><li>Thermal Flow Transducer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flow transducer market is segmented into various types including Coriolis, Mass, Ultrasonic, Vortex, and Thermal flow transducers. Coriolis flow transducers measure mass flow rate by analyzing the Coriolis forces acting on a vibrating tube. Mass flow transducers measure the mass of a substance flowing through a pipe. Ultrasonic flow transducers use ultrasonic waves to measure the flow rate of liquids or gases. Vortex flow transducers utilize vortices shed by an obstruction to measure flow rate. Thermal flow transducers measure flow rate by analyzing the temperature difference between a heated sensor and the flowing fluid.</p></p>
