<p><p>ID Card Printers market can be segmented into two main types: Dye-Sublimation Printers and Inkjet Printers. Dye-Sublimation Printers use a special printing process that transfers dye onto the card, resulting in high-quality images and durable prints. On the other hand, Inkjet Printers use liquid ink to create prints, offering a more cost-effective solution for lower volume printing needs. Both types of printers cater to different printing requirements and offer varying levels of print quality and durability.</p></p>
